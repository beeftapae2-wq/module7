📖 Three Types of Context

👤 Biographical

The author's life experiences, education, beliefs, and personal history.

Ask: What in the author's life influenced this work?

📅 Historical

Events, conditions, and ideas of the time when the story was written.

Ask: What was happening in the world then?

🌏 Sociocultural

Society, culture, traditions, beliefs, and social structures.

Ask: What cultural values are reflected?

📚 Historical Context in Literature

Understanding how the time period influences stories, themes, and values.

📖 Filipino Example: Dr. José Rizal

📜 Biographical Context

  • Born in Calamba, Laguna (1861)
  • Studied in Manila, Spain, Germany
  • His mother was wrongly imprisoned
  • Executed by Spanish colonial government

⚔️ Historical Context

  • Spanish colonization (300+ years)
  • Abuses by friars and officials
  • Filipino nationalism rising
  • Period of propaganda and reform

Impact: Rizal's novels Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo exposed colonial injustice and ignited Filipino nationalism.
