Four Types of Conflict

⚔️ Character vs. Self

Internal struggle inside a character's mind. Examples: making a difficult choice, overcoming fear, dealing with guilt.

🎬 Example: Rapunzel in Tangled

👥 Character vs. Character

Struggle between two or more characters. Examples: arguments, fights, competition.

🎬 Example: Anna vs Elsa in Frozen

🏛️ Character vs. Society

Struggle against rules, traditions, or beliefs of a group. Examples: standing up against unjust laws, breaking traditions.

🎬 Example: Mulan defying tradition

🌊 Character vs. Nature

Struggle against natural forces. Examples: surviving a storm, earthquake, wild animals, or harsh environment.

🎬 Example: Moana vs the ocean

🎯 Conflict reveals values. The choices characters make show what they truly care about.
