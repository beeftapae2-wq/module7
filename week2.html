Character Roles

🦸 Protagonist

The main character the story follows. Usually the hero or the one we root for.

Example: Mulan in Mulan

👿 Antagonist

The character or force that opposes the protagonist. Creates conflict.

Example: Shan Yu in Mulan

👥 Supporting

Characters who help, guide, or add depth to the story.

Example: Mushu in Mulan

STEAL Method: Indirect Characterization

S

Speech

What they say & how

T

Thoughts

What they think

E

Effect

How others react

A

Actions

What they do

L

Looks

Appearance

📌 Direct vs. Indirect

Direct: Author TELLS us ("Maria was kind.")

Indirect: Author SHOWS us using STEAL
