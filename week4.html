Tone vs. Mood: Quick Reference

🎙️ Tone

Author's attitude toward the subject or audience.

Examples: serious, playful, sarcastic, respectful, angry

Ask: How does the author feel about this?

🌫️ Mood

Reader's feeling or atmosphere created.

Examples: peaceful, tense, joyful, gloomy, mysterious

Ask: How does this make me feel?

Patterns & Motifs

🔁 What is a Motif?

A recurring element (image, symbol, idea) that has symbolic meaning and helps express the theme or values.

Examples: Light/dark, water, birds, colors, specific objects

✍️ Author's Style

The unique way an author writes—sentence length, word choice, use of imagery, dialogue patterns.

Think of it as the author's "fingerprint."

Activity: Motif Hunter

Think of a movie or story you love. What image or object keeps appearing? What does it represent? What value does it show?
